About Me

I like understanding how systems work from first principles.

I am a Computer Engineering / Electrical and Computer Engineering student at Bucknell University who enjoys building systems where hardware, software, math, and real-world usefulness come together.

I enjoy breaking technical topics down instead of only memorizing formulas or code. I like learning deeply, especially in math, circuits, embedded systems, and computer engineering.

I am interested in systems that connect sensors, microcontrollers, displays, cloud data, and AI. I care about knowledge, education, family, helping others, and economic return, and I want my engineering work to be both technically strong and useful.

How I Work

  • Build, test, debug, and reflect on what changed.
  • Communicate technical ideas clearly and honestly.
