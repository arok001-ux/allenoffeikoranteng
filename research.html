Research Interests

Questions I want to keep exploring

My research interests sit around edge AI, cloud AI, computer vision, embedded intelligence, and the math behind engineering systems.

Edge AI vs Cloud AI

I am interested in how real systems choose where computation should happen. Edge AI can reduce latency and dependence on a network, while Cloud AI can offer more compute and potentially stronger models.

Embedded Intelligence

I want to understand how sensors, microcontrollers, displays, communication protocols, and machine learning can work together in useful low-cost systems.

Math Behind Engineering

I enjoy connecting differential equations, linear algebra, probability, Fourier transforms, Laplace transforms, and convolution to signals, circuits, and control.

Proposal Focus

Evaluating computer vision systems on low-cost devices

My Edge AI vs Cloud AI proposal compares object classification with a Raspberry Pi and camera, using accuracy, speed, latency, and throughput as core measurements.

The expected tradeoff is practical: local inference may respond faster because it avoids network latency, while cloud inference may improve accuracy because it can use larger models and more compute.
