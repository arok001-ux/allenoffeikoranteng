MSP430
Low-level embedded C

MSP430 Reaction Timer and Internal Timer

Designed an LCD-based embedded project with reaction timer and internal timer modes using buttons, LEDs, timer logic, and an MSP430G2553 microcontroller.

What I built: A reaction timer with random delay and LED trigger, plus an internal timer mode where users estimate time intervals and compare their results on an LCD.
Hardware mapping: LCD D4-D7 on P1.0-P1.3, RS on P1.4, EN on P1.5, LEDs on P1.6/P1.7, and buttons on P2.4/P2.5.

  • GPIO
